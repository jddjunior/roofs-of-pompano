## Phase 1.3 — Competitive Landscape
Crown Roofing (multi-office regional, not owner-led), Whale Roofing (family-owned but generic), SCI/Clark/Aastro (legacy-generation storytelling), Pompano Beach Roofing Experts (an actual referral/lead-gen broker, not a contractor), MM Roof Repair (owner-fronted, repair-only).

Market-wide pattern: free estimate, licensed & insured, storm-ready, financing — hygiene factors every competitor claims. The unclaimed angle: whether you're talking to the actual license holder or a broker/rotating subcontractor. Real pricing anchor: asphalt $5,000-$10,500, metal $8,000-$30,000, 1-3 day installs.
